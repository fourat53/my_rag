The project encompasses the end-to-end creation of a scalable ecosystem, starting with a multilingual showcase website to establish a strong digital presence. The core development focused on a robust dashboard for managing clients and scripts, followed by the implementation of detailed call analytics and a real-time goal management sys- tem. To extend the platform\u2019s capabilities, essential third-party integrations with Google Workspace and AWS S3 were established, alongside a sophisticated voice management module. A central component of the work was the development of an intelligent Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) microservice, which leverages hybrid vector search and dy- namic knowledge bases to provide context-aware AI responses. in- creasing challenges in managing large volumes of customer calls while maintaining service quality and operational efficiency. Moreover, human-based call handling requires dedicated staff, generates significant operational costs and exposes companies to issues such as fatigue, inconsistency or errors during customer interactions.\n\nEcho Parrot was conceived as a response to these growing challenges. The aim of the project is to develop an intelligent VoIP solution integrating an AI-powered conversational agent capable of interacting with callers in a natural and context-aware manner. By automating the first line of call reception, the platform supports businesses that wish to optimize their telephone operations, reduce operational costs and ensure 24/7 availability without compromising the quality of interactions.\n\nThe solution provides a scalable SaaS platform designed to handle calls efficiently while adapting to the specific needs of each organization. Through advanced speech recognition, real-time natural language understanding and AI-driven decision-making, Echo Parrot processes callers\u2019 requests, extracts key information and guides interactions with preci- sion. Its cloud-based architecture ensures reliable performance and seamless integration with existing infrastructures such as CRMs, scheduling systems or document automation services.\n\nA central feature of the platform lies in its ability to transform calls into actionable insights. By analyzing conversations, detecting caller intent, identifying relevant entities and generating summaries or follow-up actions, the system enables companies to stream- line workflows and enhance customer experience. Intelligent routing, automated feedback and contextual recommendations further reinforce the platform\u2019s value, enabling teams to focus on high-impact tasks rather than repetitive or low-value activities.\n\nAdditionally, the platform addresses limitations in traditional telephone systems by offering flexibility, adaptability and intelligent traceability. Each interaction can be se- curely recorded, analyzed and enriched with metadata to support business intelligence, improve internal processes and feed existing operational tools.
