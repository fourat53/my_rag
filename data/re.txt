Guided project 3: Extract Pros and Cons from Product Reviews

Problem Statement:

Identify pros and cons from mobile phone reviews shared on an ecommerce platform

To enhance product feedback analysis, marketers and product managers must effectively review customer opinions.

This analysis allows them to identify strengths and weaknesses of the product and prioritize areas for improvement.

Imagine you are a product manager presented with a corpus of customer reviews for a newly launched mobile phone on an e-commerce platform.

Your task is to analyze these reviews and summarize key points mentioned by customers.

This summary will help refine the product experience and address any shortcomings in the critical initial days after launch.

Create a concise summary of pros and cons mentioned by customers in the reviews.

 
Input/ Dataset

    Text of reviews extracted from the commerce page of a mobile phone product: Dataset - Mobile Phone Reviews
    You can assume that this data is presented as input to the model as context along with your prompt.

Ideal prompt:

You are an expert marketer tasked with extracting insights from mobile phone product reviews.

Analyze the provided customer reviews of a newly launched mobile phone to summarize the key points, focusing on both positive aspects (pros) and negative aspects (cons).

Guidelines:

- Focus on identifying patterns or recurring themes in customer feedback.

- Separate the feedback into two categories: "Pros" and "Cons."

- Be concise and highlight only the key issues or praises frequently mentioned across the reviews.

- Avoid including isolated or unique issues unless they are significantly impactful to product quality or usability.

- Maintain objectivity and avoid rewording subjective opinions into definitive statements unless supported by multiple similar reviews.

Use the following steps in your analysis

    Extract Key Points from Customer Reviews:

   - Skim through provided reviews to identify commonly discussed aspects of the product.

   - Note down specific phrases or topics frequently highlighted as positives (pros), such as features, performance, or design.

   - Similarly, identify areas criticized or considered weaknesses (cons), such as bugs, missing features, or usability challenges.

    Categorize Pros and Cons:

   - Separate the collected feedback into two categories: Pros and Cons.

   - Rank the points within each category by frequency of mention, ensuring the most common observations are prioritized.

    Summarize:

   - Write short, clear summaries for both pros and cons. Avoid redundancy or over-detailed descriptions.

    Format the Output:

   - The final output should be in a structured format with labeled sections for "Pros" and "Cons" for clarity.

The output should be formatted as follows in plain text:

Pros:

    [Key point summarizing a positive aspect of the product.]
    [Additional key point(s) as needed.]

Cons:

    [Key point summarizing a negative aspect of the product.]
    [Additional key point(s) as needed.]

The total number of points in each category should be manageable and focused on key recurring themes (ideally 3-5 points for each).
